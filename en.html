It's the optimization of your site's structure and code so it's well understood and indexed by Google. This includes:

  • Images: Descriptive alt attributes
  • Performance: Fast loading (< 2 sec)
  • Responsive: Adapted for mobile and tablet

The technical SEO I implement prepares your site for Google, but ranking depends on several factors:

  • Competition: How many other sites in your sector?
  • Time: Natural SEO takes 3-6 months minimum
  • Content: Quality and quantity of your texts
  • Authority: External links, domain age

What I guarantee: Your site will be structured, fast and "SEO-ready". But I cannot guarantee a specific position.
